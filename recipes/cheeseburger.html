Cheesebuger

Description

This recipe will show you how to make a delicious Cheeseburger!

Perfect for a mid week meal because it only takes about 15 minutes to prepare and cook!

Ingredients

  • 600g Beef Mince
  • 1/8 tsp each salt and pepper per burger
  • 2-3 tbsp Oil
  • 4 Soft Burger Buns
  • 4 - 8 Slices Burger Cheese
  • 8 Lettuce Leaves
  • 2 Large Tomatoes
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 2 Large Gherkins
  • Burger sauce of your choice.

Steps

  1. Patties - Seperate the beef into 4 equal portions and roll each portion into balls and press down till they are flat.
  2. Prepare: Get all your burbger bits and pieces out and ready to use. Don't salt your beef until just before cooking (it toughens the meat).
  3. Toast Buns: Preheat the grill to high/ Place the breat cut face up on a trat and toast for 3 to 5 minutes until light golden.
  4. Dent Patty: Sprinkle one side of patties half the salt and pepper. Flip, then press a dent into the middle of each patty. Sprinkle this dented side with the remaining alt and pepper.
  5. Preheat 1 tablespoon oil in a large cast iron skillet over high heat until very hot - smoking hot!
  6. Cook: Cook 2 to 4 patties for 1 1/2 minutes. Press down lightly with a spatula. Flip, then immediately top with a slice of cheese. Cook for another 1 1.2 minutes then transfer burgers to a tray to rest for 3 minutes.
  7. Slather the base of the bun with your sauce of choice and stack all of the ingredients along with the patty inside of your bun.
  8. Devour with french fries on the side.
