One Pot Creamy Pasta

This recipe will show you how to make a delicious Creamy One Pot Pasta dish.

Save on the washing up with this amazing one pot pasta dish!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Minced
  • 1 Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 500g Mince Beef
  • 2 tbsp Italian Herbs
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 500g Crushed Tomatoes
  • 0.5 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1.5 tsp Cooking Salt
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1l Chicken Stock/Broth
  • 350g Penne Pasta
  • 180ml Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Saute - Heat the oil on high heat in a large heavy-based pot. Cook garlic and onion for 1 1/2 minutes.
  2. Cook beef and seasonings - Add beef and cook, breaking it up as you go, until you can no longer see red meat. Add the Italian herbs and cook for 30 seconds, then add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute to cook out the raw flavour.
  3. All in - Add crushed tomato, chicken stock, salt, pepper and red pepper flakes, if using. Stir, the add the pasta.
  4. Cook 15 minutes - Bring the liquid to a simmer, then cook for 15 minutes, stirring every couple of minutes then more frequently towards the end (ensure pasta doesn't stick to base) until the pasta is just about cooked.
  5. Creamy - Add cream, then simmer for a further 1 to 2 minutes. It will still be quite saucy â€“ this is what you want! Pasta absorbs liquid quickly, so it will still be nice and oozy when you start eating.
  6. Serve - Remove from the stove. Give it a good stir then ladle into bowls. Server with parmesan and parsley.
